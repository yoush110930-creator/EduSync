# Privacy Policy – EduSync

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026

EduSync is committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency regarding data collection, use, and storage.

---

## 1. Data Collected

### Content Data
- Text provided by users for **Academic Optimization (Humanizer)** is sent to [https://undetectai.io/](https://undetectai.io/) and [https://cleverhumanizer.ai/](https://cleverhumanizer.ai/) for processing.  
- Text entered into **Auto-Typing (Transcription Emulator)** is handled entirely **locally** in the browser.  

**Note:** EduSync does **not** collect personal information such as emails, usernames, or identifiers. Auto-Typing text is **not transmitted externally**.

### Technical / Usage Data
- Anonymous usage metrics (feature clicks, frequency) for debugging and performance improvement  
- Browser type and extension version  

No browsing history, page URLs, or other personal data is collected.

---

## 2. How Data Is Used

- **Humanizer:** Text is processed to provide refined output using https://cleverhumanizer.ai/ and https://undetectai.io/.  
- **Auto-Typing:** Text is typed locally; no external processing occurs.  
- Anonymous metrics are used solely to improve extension performance and stability.  

EduSync does **not** sell, rent, or share user data for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- **Humanizer:** Text may be temporarily processed on the external API server, but EduSync does **not** store it permanently.  
- **Auto-Typing:** Text is processed locally; no server storage occurs.  
- All usage data and logs are **deleted immediately after processing**.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

- EduSync relies on [https://undetectai.io/](https://undetectai.io/) and [https://cleverhumanizer.ai/](https://cleverhumanizer.ai/) for text refinement (Humanizer). This service operates under its own terms and privacy policies.  
- No other third-party integrations or analytics are used.  
- Auto-Typing is fully local; no external services are involved.

---

## 5. Cookies / Tracking

- EduSync does **not** use cookies or tracking scripts.  
- All user interactions are processed locally or anonymously.

---

## 6. User Rights (GDPR – EU Users)

Users in the EU have the right to:

- Access any personal data collected (none is collected)  
- Request deletion (all data is deleted after use)  
- Object to processing (no personal data processing occurs)  
- Data portability requests  

---

## 7. User Rights (CCPA – California Residents)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected (none)  
- Request deletion (data is deleted immediately after processing)  
- Opt out of sale (EduSync does **not** sell data)  
- Receive equal service regardless of exercising privacy rights  

---

## 8. Security Measures

EduSync implements:

- Secure connections (HTTPS) for Humanizer API requests  
- Local processing for Auto-Typing to minimize data transmission  
- Minimal data retention to enhance security  
- Secure coding practices in all extension components  

---

## 9. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

- EduSync will promptly investigate and contain the incident  
- Users will be notified if any personal data is affected, in accordance with applicable laws  

---

## 10. Children’s Privacy

EduSync is **not intended for children under 13** and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 11. Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of EduSync constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
